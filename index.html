Dairy farming is a form of agriculture that is dedicated to the production of milk and
dairy products from the care and feeding of cattle, mainly dairy cows.
Dairy farmers focus on managing the health, welfare, and
milk production of animals to obtain the highest quantity and quality of milk possible.
Dairy production provides many non-marketed economic benefits, including manure for use on-farm as fuel or organic fertilizer
(in several farming systems manure is the sole source of nutrients for crop production).
